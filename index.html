About

We build a culture of real olive oil appreciation.

Olio Del Nonno is a team of tasters, farmers, and culinary advisors. We travel the Mediterranean, look for bold character olives, and preserve their story in every bottle.

Craft-first philosophy

We do not chase mass production. Instead, we create limited batches where we knows the harvest and we know the flavor profile. Every batch has a certificate of origin and a transparent story.

Our journey

Each milestone is daily work and care for quality.

1975

First partnerships

We found family farms in Italy that agreed to full transparency.

Our own lab

Collection

Olive oil with distinct flavor profiles for different dishes.

We created a line with different levels of bitterness and spice so you can match an oil to your cooking style.

Cuvee Verde

Koroneiki from early harvest. Notes of herbs, green apple peel, and fresh almond.

  • Intensity: medium
  • Best for: salads, tartare, fish
  • Acidity: 0.25%

Gold Harvest

Arbequina and Leccino blend for a silky texture. Soft sweetness and ripe nut finish.

  • Intensity: delicate
  • Best for: pasta, vegetables, light sauces
  • Acidity: 0.18%

Noire Reserve

Late harvest from Andalusia. Spicy notes of black pepper, artichoke, and bitter cacao.

  • Intensity: high
  • Best for: grill, meat, rich soups
  • Acidity: 0.30%
