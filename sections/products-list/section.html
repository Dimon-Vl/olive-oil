Cuvee Verde

Koroneiki from early harvest. Notes of herbs, green apple peel, and fresh almond.

  • Intensity: medium
  • Best for: salads, tartare, fish
  • Acidity: 0.25%

Gold Harvest

Arbequina and Leccino blend for a silky texture. Soft sweetness and ripe nut finish.

  • Intensity: delicate
  • Best for: pasta, vegetables, light sauces
  • Acidity: 0.18%

Noire Reserve

Late harvest from Andalusia. Spicy notes of black pepper, artichoke, and bitter cacao.

  • Intensity: high
  • Best for: grill, meat, rich soups
  • Acidity: 0.30%
